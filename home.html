How It Works ?

Team

Do you feel like the brainstorming process has become boring nowadays ? Or perhaps are you clueless and confused about what to do for your next project, what techs to use or maybe where to hangout with your friends ? Thatâ€™s what Ideation is here for !


  1. Set your brainstorming prompt and simply type in the ideas in a given time.


  2. If more than one users enter the same idea, the word becomes bigger in the word cloud.


  3. Anyone can start a vote by clicking on an idea from the idea log. You can either choose not to vote by not clicking anything during the voting phase or choose to Vote.


  4. If majority votes for the same word, the word is decided and forms a thought bubble ! As you keep on ideating, you can see your thought process on the right side of your screen. You can also branch out of a voted idea and start brainstorming more about that idea. If you hover over a cloud, you can see the prompt for that cloud.


  5. Be careful as you have a countdown timer during both the brainstorming phase and voting phase !
